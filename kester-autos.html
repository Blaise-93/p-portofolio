Project Overview

Everything in the world is moving into the internet, and businesses and organizations are not an exception. The COVID-19 that happened 2019 created the surge of Ecommerce website and it has really played a significant role in market capitalization. KesterAutos which is an Ecommerce website is not going to be left behind and that's why it has come to leverage on the internet visibility for it's market audience.

KesterAutos is a medium-scale ecommerce website that handles user registration, authenticate them for each product order the user made, allow them to make payment via Paystack or book inspection date in real time. It is perfectly designed in with nice UI/UX. It also leverages on Django inbuilt admin interface to handle admin related database manipulation with PostgresQL.

Some features KesterAutos offers:

  • Landing page with sections like About, Team, Footer, Newsletter Subscription for online marketing and aggregating of clients' lookup/requests.
  • E-commerce page (Buy-online) with UX on how to buy any products of your client choice with easy navigations by your clients.
  • Payment gateway usage -> Paystack, a subsidiary of Stripe is used here to enable customers purchase their chosen product with their payment seing by our admin in real-time
  • Life cycle of the business is encapsulated in this pattern -> Order : Payment Received: Being Deliverd : Refund Requested : Refund Granted
  • Email marketing and collection and usage from admin via Sendgrid is highly used here with their API.
  • Contact us functionality is added to help us with 24/7 customer care services with our clients because KesterAutos is customer-oriented service.

Built with Python/Django, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, SCSS, CSS and HTML, Unittest (for unittesting and integration testing). Other tools used are Sendgrid for email marketing, Git/Github, Railway for web hosting at the moment, we are planningon moving to DigitalOcean soon.
